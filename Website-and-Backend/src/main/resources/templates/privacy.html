Ethicli collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s), browser extension, and deliver the services you have requested. Ethicli may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Ethicli and its affiliates.

For information on how your information is used or shared with third parties see the section below titled “Sharing Information with Third Parties”.
