About Ethicli

We are a team of developers who want to change the world by bringing you greater consumer power! One of the best ways to vote for what you believe in is with your dollars. Many of us can feel lost when it comes to knowing how our purchases affect others downstream, and we're here to help!

Read our Pitch Deck

We show you ethical scores of online shops based on how they treat their workers, community, and the planet. Please use our free browser extension to effortlessly shop from more ethical, green, and smaller businesses!

How to use Ethicli

How it Works

While shopping, click on the Ethicli icon in your browser menu to see a breakdown of a shop’s score. Want to know more? Select “View Details” to access links, sources, and suggestions for better products!

Sometimes while shopping, we'll recommend more ethical products based on what you're shopping for.

About Ratings

We use a data-driven algorithm based on companies' certifications and partnerships to score online shops, saving you the time and energy it takes to be sure your purchase reflects your values.

Info Page

After clicking the "View Details" button on the extension, a new tab opens with score-breakdowns and details of how and why each online business recieved their scores.
