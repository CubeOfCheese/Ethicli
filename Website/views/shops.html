Girlfriend Collective

Girlfriend Collective has model diversity and an extensive circular program that buys back their used items (made from recycled BPA-free water bottles) to make new ones! They operate in a SA8000-certified zero-waste facility, are size-inclusive (XXS—6XL), anti-child labor, and vegan.

Visit Website

Uncommon Goods

Uncommon Goods who rates a 9.5 out of 10. They pay crafters and artisans a living wage to sell all kinds of ethical handmade items. With such a robust selection of unique goods, we recommend this shop as one of your first stops for your holiday shopping this year!

Visit Website

Made Trade

Made Trade is a woman and POC-owned marketplace of sustainable, fair trade, hand-made, USA-made, and vegan products. They also donate to 1% for the Planet, and have a Carbon-Neutral checkout process.

Visit Website

Tru Earth

Tru Earth is a cruelty-free, palm oil free laundry brand that shares their ingredients transparently on their website. They’re best known for their waterless, vegan detergent strips!

Visit Website

Pela

Pela products are compostable & ship in plastic-free packaging. They are Climate Neutral certified, and also donate to 1% for the Planet!

Visit Website

Babo Botanicals

Babo Botanicals is a family-centered skincare company focused on products for babies, parents, and everyone in between.

Visit Website

Sheets & Giggles

Sheets & Giggles makes sustainable bedding (sheets, comforters, pillowcases, blankets, etc.) from eucalyptus wood pulp, grown and harvested on biodiverse farms. For every Eucalyptus tree harvested, they plant two more!
